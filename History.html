HISTORY OF WOW-MARKET



WOW-MARKET is a Limited Liability Company. WOW-MARKET was started by Jennisha Smith, Lemard Sterling, Altheia Robinson, Shani Parchment and Jayden Barham in the year 2021 to meet the demands in the market that were not being produced in good quality at an affordable price to consumers. One-Stop Market was the first name of the business it was then changed to WOW-MARKET in 2021. It is located at 28 Manchester Road, Manchester Jamaica, West Indies. From ever since, the founders had always wanted to start their own market centre knowing the struggles poor persons go through with products being too expensive.



After they obtained the necessary qualifications in the business field, they invested in purchasing land to start the venture. However, they experienced challenges at the beginning of the venture with competitors which resulted in the reduction of sales. WOW-MARKET in an aid to remedy the situation they conducted a survey and realized that the competitor did not have a consistent price at the same time they did not have islandwide delivery, resulting in Wow-Mart having the advantage.The business is in the secondary sector and has expanded into several parishes island wide in Jamaica. The business was established in the late 2000’s which operates within a mixed economy. It is the one of the first online shopping store to provide all necessary tools/products for both gender at any age.



Wow-Mart offers a wide variety of products which include electronics, clothing, drugs, make-up tools, among others. Altogether, the business has approximately 200 employees. The business targets mostly young personalities (ages 16-45), and imports goods from top brands such as Nike, and Romewe. Wow-Mart does not have a main competitor within the industry. This is because most items such as make-up tools and accessories in Jamaica are being sold in wholesale and retail stores only. Wow-Mart is currently in the phase of expanding to larger countries; to further reach individuals. In addition, we have created an online presence to capture users that are always engaged in social media sites to showcase our brand.



this new phase we are creating a business so that customers can be part of the developmental process an idea pool will be established where customers can pitch their ideas to the company to aid in new product line. As owners of such a successful business, they aspire to continue in growth and development for years to come.
